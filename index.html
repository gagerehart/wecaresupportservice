About Us

WeCare Support Services was created by a nurse with over 15 years of experience and a deep passion for helping others live with dignity and support. Inspired by a calling to serve those who need it most, Sarah brings compassion, care, and a personal touch to every service offered.

Our Mission

At WeCare Support Services, our mission is to provide compassionate, dependable, and personalized support to seniors and individuals with developmental or cognitive challenges. We are committed to enhancing quality of life by offering essential services that promote independence, dignity, and peace of mind for families.

Services Offered

  • Transportation to medical appointments
  • Grocery shopping and delivery
  • Medication reminders
  • Social activity planning
  • YMCA/exercise support
  • Daily check-ins or wellness calls
  • Companion visits
  • Light errand running
